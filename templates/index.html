Core Features

What can JillAI do for you?

Web Searches Made Easy

With JillAI, accessing information has never been simpler. Just type your query, and watch as JillAI scours the web to bring you the most relevant results in seconds. Say goodbye to endless searching and hello to instant answers!

Stay Updated with the Weather

Never be caught off guard by the elements again! JillAI provides real-time weather updates, ensuring you're always prepared for what’s ahead. Whether it’s a sunny day or a rainy forecast, JillAI keeps you in the loop.

Seamless Event Scheduling

Organizing your life has never been easier! JillAI integrates effortlessly with Google Calendar, allowing you to schedule events and set reminders by simply telling JillAI to do it. Keep your schedule on track and never miss an important moment again.

Customizable Personas for Your Needs

Choose from over eight unique personas, each designed to help people who are as unique as their personal assistants should be. From productivity boosting to wellness guides and emotional support, JillAI’s personas adapt to your preferences, ensuring you get the support you need when you need it.
